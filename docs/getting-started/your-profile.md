# Your Profile

The Profile page is your personal hub on Mintware. It shows your Attribution score, wallet history, and referral status.

---

## Profile Tabs

### Portfolio
A summary of your on-chain asset positions and protocol deployments — where your capital is currently sitting and how it's allocated.

### Score
Your full Attribution breakdown:
- Total score and tier
- Signal-by-signal breakdown with insights
- Score improvement opportunities
- EAS attestation (publish your score on-chain)

### Badge
Your wallet character and tier badge. Shareable proof of your Attribution standing.

### Invite
Your referral code and link. Share it to grow your Sharing signal score and earn referral rewards when people you invite participate in campaigns.

---

## Wallet Display

Where possible, Mintware resolves your wallet address to a **Basename** (Base Name Service handle). If you have a Basename registered on Base, it will display throughout the app instead of your raw address.

---

## Score Attestation

From the Score tab, you can publish your Attribution score as an EAS attestation. Once attested:

- Your score is permanently recorded on the EAS network
- Third parties can verify your score without relying on Mintware's API
- The attestation is linked to your wallet address and signed by Mintware's oracle
