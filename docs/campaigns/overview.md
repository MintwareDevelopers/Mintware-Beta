# Campaign Overview

Campaigns are the core reward mechanism on Mintware. Protocols create campaigns to incentivise specific user behaviour — trading, bridging, providing liquidity, or referring others.

Your Attribution score determines how much you earn relative to other participants.

---

## Finding Campaigns

The **Dashboard** (`/dashboard`) lists all available campaigns. You can filter by:

- **Status** — Live, Upcoming, Ended
- **Chain** — Base, Core DAO, BNB, and others

Each campaign card shows:
- Campaign name and sponsor
- Reward pool size and token
- Status and time remaining
- Qualifying actions

---

## Two Campaign Types

| | Token Reward Pool | Points Campaign |
|---|---|---|
| **Created by** | Anyone | Whitelisted teams only |
| **Reward trigger** | Per qualifying swap | Per epoch (time window) |
| **Score multiplier** | No | Yes |
| **Access** | Open | May require minimum score |

See [Token Reward Pools](token-reward-pools.md) and [Points Campaigns](points-campaigns.md) for full detail.

---

## Campaign Detail Page

Click any campaign to open its detail page. You'll see:

- Full campaign description and rules
- Your current participation status
- Qualifying actions and how many you've completed
- Current leaderboard for this campaign
- Reward pool status and time to next epoch

---

## Leaderboard

The **Leaderboard** (`/leaderboard`) shows rankings across campaigns. Select a campaign to see where you stand relative to other participants.
