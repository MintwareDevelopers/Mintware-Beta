# Joining a Campaign

---

## Steps to Join

1. Go to the **Dashboard** (`/dashboard`)
2. Find a campaign you want to join — filter by status or chain if needed
3. Click the campaign to open its detail page
4. Review the qualifying actions and reward structure
5. Click **Join Campaign**
6. Confirm the wallet signature

That's it. You're now a participant. Start completing qualifying actions to earn points or trigger swap rewards.

---

## Eligibility

**Token Reward Pools** — open to any connected wallet.

**Points Campaigns** — may require a minimum Attribution score. If your score is below the threshold, the Join button will be disabled and your current score will be shown alongside the requirement.

---

## After Joining

Once joined, the campaign appears in your dashboard with your current participation status. You'll see:
- Points earned this epoch (Points Campaigns)
- Pending rewards (Token Reward Pools)
- Your rank on the campaign leaderboard

---

## Multiple Campaigns

You can join as many campaigns as you like. There's no limit.
