# Score Tiers

Your Attribution score determines your tier. Tiers affect your eligibility for certain campaigns and the multiplier applied to your Points Campaign earnings.

---

## Tiers

| Tier | Score Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| **Bronze** | 0 – 299 | Starting tier for most wallets |
| **Silver** | 300 – 599 | Established on-chain presence |
| **Gold** | 600 – 925 | Long-term, high-quality participants |

> Score ranges are indicative. The exact boundaries are set by the Attribution engine and may be adjusted as the scoring model evolves.

---

## Percentile

Your profile also shows your **percentile** — where your score sits relative to all scored wallets. This gives you a sense of your standing beyond the tier label.

---

## How to Improve Your Score

Your score reflects the breadth and quality of your on-chain history. The following types of activity are measured:

- **Consistent activity** over time — not just recent bursts
- **Cross-chain presence** — activity across multiple networks
- **Trading and holding behaviour** — long-term conviction is rewarded
- **Liquidity provision** — participating in protocol liquidity
- **Governance participation** — voting and on-chain governance
- **Referrals** — bringing quality wallets into your network

There is no shortcut. Attribution is designed to be resistant to manufactured activity.

---

## Score Opportunities

Your profile's **Score tab** surfaces personalised protocol opportunities based on your current signal gaps — protocols where your existing profile is a strong fit, with estimated score ranges you could achieve by participating.
