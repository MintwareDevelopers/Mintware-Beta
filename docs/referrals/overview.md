# Referral System

Mintware has a built-in referral system that rewards you for bringing quality wallets into the platform.

---

## How It Works

1. Find your referral link on your **Profile → Invite tab**
2. Share it with people you want to invite
3. When they connect their wallet via your link, they're recorded as your referral
4. When your referrals participate in campaigns, you earn referral rewards

---

## Your Referral Code

Every wallet gets a permanent referral code. New wallets get a code based on their **Basename** (Base Name Service handle) if they have one — for example, if your Basename is `jake`, your code is `jake`.

If you don't have a Basename, a short code is generated from your wallet address.

Your code never changes once assigned.

---

## Referral Link Format

```
mintware.xyz/ref/YOUR_CODE
```

Share this link directly. When someone clicks it and connects their wallet on Mintware for the first time, the referral is recorded.

---

## Referral Rewards

Referral rewards vary by campaign. In Points Campaigns, you can earn points when your referred wallets complete qualifying actions:

- When a referred wallet bridges, you earn **referral bridge points**
- When a referred wallet trades, you earn **referral trade points**

The exact point values depend on the campaign configuration.

---

## Sharing Score

Your referral activity contributes to your **Sharing** Attribution signal. A larger, higher-quality referral network improves your Sharing score — which in turn improves your overall Attribution score and multiplier.

---

## Referral Eligibility

To be counted as a valid referral, your wallet must have been active on Mintware for at least 24 hours before the referred wallet connects. This prevents fresh accounts from pre-seeding referral chains.
