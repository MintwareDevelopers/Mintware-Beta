# Your Referral Code

---

## Finding Your Code

1. Connect your wallet
2. Go to **Profile** (`/profile`)
3. Click the **Invite** tab
4. Your referral link is displayed — click to copy

---

## Code Format

Referral codes are human-readable where possible:

- **Basename holders** — your code is your Basename handle (e.g. `jake` → `mintware.xyz/ref/jake`)
- **Everyone else** — a short alphanumeric code derived from your wallet address

---

## Sharing Your Link

You can share your referral link anywhere:
- Directly in messages
- On Twitter / X
- In Discord servers
- In any community where you want to invite people

Anyone who connects their wallet via your link for the first time will be counted as your referral.

---

## Tracking Referrals

The Invite tab shows your referral network stats:
- Total referrals
- Network size (direct + downstream)
- Your Sharing score contribution

These stats update in real-time as new wallets join via your link.
